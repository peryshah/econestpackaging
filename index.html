ABOUT ECONEST

Sustainable Packaging for a Greener Tomorrow

EcoNest Packaging is committed to providing high-quality sugarcane bagasse food packaging solutions that combine sustainability, durability and elegant presentation. Our products are designed for restaurants, cafés, hotels, cloud kitchens, caterers and food service businesses looking to replace single-use plastic with environmentally responsible alternatives.

We work with certified manufacturing partners to deliver premium quality products with consistent supply, competitive pricing and private labelling options. Our long-term vision is to establish EcoNest as one of India's leading sustainable food packaging brands.
